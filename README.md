​CSL-LLM: Chip Simulation-to-Tapeout Large Language Model

​"In the 3nm era, we are no longer designing circuits; we are encoding the DNA of resilient digital life."

​1. Abstract
​The CSL-LLM (Chip Simulation-to-Tapeout Large Language Model) is a foundational architectural framework designed to address the catastrophic physical entropy of the 3nm/2nm process nodes. It bridges the gap between probabilistic generative AI and deterministic semiconductor physics, enabling Physical Sovereignty and Autonomous Hardware Self-Healing.

​Traditional EDA flows treat design and verification as discrete steps. CSL-LLM unifies them into a continuous, generative evolutionary loop.

## 🌌 Cross-Domain Vision: From Silicon to Aerospace

The CSL-LLM paradigm is not limited to semiconductors. Its core philosophy—**Physical Sovereignty via Autonomous Self-Healing**—is directly transferable to extreme macro-engineering, such as **Orbital Rocket Recovery**.

### 🚀 Case Study: Next-Gen Aerodynamic Deceleration
Traditional rocket recovery relies on static flight software. A CSL-LLM enabled flight controller would:
* **PAF (Physical-Aware Flight):** Pre-encode fluid dynamics and thermal ionization constants into the hardware logic.
* **Kinetic Self-Healing:** If a grid fin is damaged by hypersonic heating, the **ChaKou Anchor** bypasses the failed actuator logic in nanoseconds, re-routing control to engine gimbals based on "Physical Heartbeat" rather than software commands.
* **CTC Stabilization:** Utilizing Centripetal-Tuning-Cascade to maintain the rocket's center of mass (CoM) equilibrium during violent aerodynamic braking.

​2.The Pillars of Physical Sovereignty
​This repository defines the core specification for the three layers of the CSL-LLM architecture:

I. PAG (Physical-Aware Generation)
​Generates RTL code that is inherently aware of sub-3nm physical constraints, including:
​Gate-All-Around (GAA) leakage profiles.
​Quantum tunneling mitigation.
​Metal interconnect electromigration modeling.

​II. SPF (Simulation Pressure Feedback)
​An autonomous adversarial simulation engine that:
​Injects "Hard Failures" (transistor burnout, thermal hotspots) during the RTL/Gate-level simulation.
​Forces the LLM to generate Self-Healing Re-routing Logic before GDSII finalization.

​III. ChaKou (叉口) Physical Anchor
​The final hardware implementation of the "Physical Lock" and "Autonomous Recovery Path."
​Atomic-Level Gates: Hard-wired gates that respond in nanoseconds to physical breaches or failures.
​Independence: Operates entirely at the hardware layer, independent of the OS or high-level software stack.

​3. The CTC System (Centripetal-Tuning-Cascade)
​
The CSL-LLM framework is powered by the CTC System, a proprietary methodology for ensuring centripetal physical equilibrium across billion-transistor arrays. CTC ensures that the hardware remains functional even when 15-20% of its physical units encounter critical failure.
